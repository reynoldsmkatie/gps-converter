GPS Coordinates Converter:
DD to DMS

The most common formats of GPS coordinates are DD (Decimal Degrees) and DMS (Degrees, Minutes, Seconds).

DD Example: 47.527859, -120.821021
  Positive left value = North
  Negative left value = South
  Positive right value = East
  Negative right value = West

DMS Example: 47° 31' 40" N, 120° 49' 15" W
  Left side = Latitute, right side = Longitude
